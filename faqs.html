General Questions

What is an air fryer, and how does it work?

An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that cooks food by circulating hot air around it, resulting in crispy and delicious meals with less oil than traditional frying methods.

Are AFK air fryers safe to use?

Yes, all our air fryers meet rigorous safety standards and are equipped with features such as auto shut-off and cool-touch handles for safe operation.

Usage and Maintenance

How do I clean my air fryer?

Cleaning your air fryer is simple. Remove the basket and pan, wash them with warm, soapy water, and wipe down the interior with a damp cloth.

Can I cook frozen food in an air fryer?

Yes! Air fryers are perfect for cooking frozen foods like fries, chicken nuggets, and fish sticks. They cook quickly and evenly without the need for defrosting.

Product Features

What is the capacity of AFK air fryers?

Our air fryers come in various sizes, ranging from 4.5 quarts for small families to 8 quarts for larger households.

Do AFK air fryers have a warranty?

Yes, all our air fryers come with a one-year warranty. Extended warranties are also available for purchase.

Troubleshooting

Why is my air fryer not turning on?

Ensure that the power cord is securely plugged in, and the basket is properly inserted. If the issue persists, contact our customer support team.

What should I do if my air fryer smokes during cooking?

This can happen if there is excess oil or food debris in the basket. Clean the basket and pan thoroughly before cooking.
